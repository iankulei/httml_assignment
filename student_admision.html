Sacho High School - Junior High Admission

Sacho High School is excited to announce admissions for students joining Junior High School. Join our esteemed institution to begin a journey of academic excellence and holistic growth.

Qualifications:

  • A minimum grade of B+ in the most recent national examinations.
  • Certified copies of the student's academic certificates.
  • A copy of the birth certificate.

Required Details:

  • Student's full name and date of birth.
  • Parent's or guardian's full name and contact details.
  • Permanent residential address.

Interview Scheduling:

Qualified applicants will be invited for an interview to assess readiness for Junior High School. The interview schedule will be shared within one week of receiving the completed application.
